Privacy Policy

Overview

Like many websites, Apparebit does not collect any information beyond tracking how you engage with its content and whether you share content on social networks. This privacy policy specifies what data is collected for user engagement, what data is collected for social sharing, and how that data is managed.

Tracking User Engagement

To better understand how you engage with my website, I use Google Analytics. As detailed in Google’s Privacy Policy, this tool may collect information about (1) your device, including hardware model and operating system, (2) your location on the internet (eg, IP address) and in the physical world (eg, GPS coordinates), and (3) your timing, including how long you wait for a page to load and how long you stay on the page thereafter.

Google Analytics offers several configuration options. Notably, this website does not collect any data for “Remarketing” or “Advertising Reporting Features.” Similarly, it does not utilize the “User-ID” feature, which can track user engagement across devices. Finally, it uses a data retention period of 14 months, which is the shortest possible period at the time of this writing.

In collecting the above metrics, Google Analytics does store some data on your computer as “cookies” or in comparable formats. You can opt-out of data collection for all websites using Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Tracking Social Sharing

This website integrates with other websites, notably social networks, only minimally. In particular, it does not utilize any third-party resources, be they content or services, with exception of Google Analytics as disclosed above.

This website does, however, link to content on other websites including social networks. This privacy policy does not apply, if you follow such outbound links. Furthermore, this website does contain identifiers for my social network accounts on Facebook and Twitter. If you share content from this website to either social network, that network tracks your activity and makes aggregate information available to me.

Please consult the privacy policies for Facebook and Twitter for further detail.

Managing Collected Data

The user engagement data for web pages is stored on Google’s systems and the social sharing data is stored on Facebook’s as well as Twitter’s systems. I am the only user authorized for data access and report generation. I may, however, share reports with select third parties, as becomes necessary for development and operation of this site.
