Apparebit

Hello,

I am Robert Grimm and I welcome you to Apparebit, my personal website. Given a professional career spanning both academic computer science and industrial software engineering, technology is an important topic here. Having additional experience in social work, gay activism, movie making, and art photography, you will find that I also take a strong interest in arts and politics. Independent of topic, my goal is to tell compelling stories—whether they are told through words, photographs, or code. After all, story telling is essential for humans connecting with each other and learning from each other.

With that in mind, I invite you to savor what’s already here and hope you’ll come back for more. I am in the process of migrating content from previous websites and Facebook—thus consolidating my online presence here. I am also working on new material. If you have a question or comment, please do reach out and say Hello, too!

Robert Grimm
