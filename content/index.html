Welcome

Hello, I am Robert Grimm. Welcome to my personal website Apparebit. Given a professional career spanning both academic computer science and industrial software engineering, you can expect computing and technology to be important topics. But having significant other experience, including in social work, gay activism, film making, and art photography, politics and arts also are fair game. Behind all these topics is a drive to tell compelling stories—whether told through words, photographs, or code. After all, story telling is essential for humans connecting with each other and learning from each other.

With that in mind, I invite you to savor what’s already here and hope you’ll come back for more. I am in the process of migrating content from previous websites and Facebook—thus consolidating my online presence here. I’m always working on new material as well. If you have a question or comment, please do reach out and say Hello, too!
